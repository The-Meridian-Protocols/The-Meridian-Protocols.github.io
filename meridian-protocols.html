Protocols

The Meridian offers two categories of listening protocol — each designed for a different state of body and mind. Both use binaural audio. The difference is in how you show up to listen.

If you are new here, read both descriptions below before choosing. The right protocol depends not on preference, but on what your body is doing when you press play.

Choose Your Protocol

AL

Active Listening

For focused, upright activity

Use while actively doing something — reading, writing, journaling, or working. Your body is upright, your mind is engaged.

  • Seated or standing
  • Eyes open
  • Active mental engagement
  • Stereo headphones recommended
Explore AL →
RL

Relaxed Listening

For meditation & deep internal states

Use while lying down or sitting in meditation. Your body is still, your focus is internal, and the protocol guides you deeper.

  • Lying down or meditation posture
  • Eyes closed or eye mask
  • No active tasks
  • Stereo headphones required

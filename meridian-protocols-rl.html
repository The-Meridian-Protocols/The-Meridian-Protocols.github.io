Relaxed Listening Protocols

About Relaxed Listening

For meditation and deep internal states — use these while lying down or sitting in stillness.

Your body is still, your focus is internal, and the protocol guides you into deeper states of consciousness.

  • Lying down or comfortable meditation posture
  • Eyes closed or eye mask
  • No active tasks
  • Stereo headphones required
Do not use while
  • Driving or operating machinery
  • If you have epilepsy or a seizure disorder
  • If you are pregnant — consult your physician first
  • If you use a pacemaker or implanted medical device

Heart Coherence

The heart has its own nervous system — thousands of neurons, sometimes called the "heart-brain," which send signals upward as well as receiving them. When the heart settles into a steady, coherent rhythm, many listeners report clearer perception, steadier attention, and a smoother sense of flow.

Why 7.83 Hz?

7.83 Hz is the Earth's natural resonance frequency (the Schumann resonance) — sitting between Alpha and Theta: calm but awake, receptive but present, centered but ready. This frequency is designed to encourage grounded awareness and creative readiness, and many listeners describe a sense of settling into a steady, receptive rhythm.

How to Use

  1. Sit upright and comfortably with stereo headphones
  2. Set volume low and gentle
  3. Place one hand over your heart
  4. Set your intention: "I choose to create from my heart. I allow coherence to lead."
  5. Breathe slowly — let attention rest in your chest
  6. Allow the sound to guide you inward without forcing imagery

After the Session

This protocol is a launch point, not the destination. Within 5–30 minutes after — write, draw, create, speak, or build. Do not check your phone. Create directly from the state you just activated.

What to Expect

First uses
  • Subtle calming
  • Slight shift of attention
  • Increased presence
With practice
  • Faster coherence entry
  • Less mental interference
  • More authentic expression
Over time
  • Creation feels cleaner
  • Decisions feel simpler
  • The heart leads naturally

Most creation comes from thought.
This protocol teaches creation from coherence.
Not from analysis. Not from polarity. Not from effort.
From wholeness. The heart already knows.

Theta Portal

Purpose

Designed for

Not intended for

Why 6.5 Hz?

Theta brainwaves naturally occur in deep meditation, REM sleep, hypnagogic states, and during creative insight. At 6.5 Hz — often described as a mind-awake / body-asleep state — this frequency is associated with subconscious access, emotional processing, memory integration, and intuitive perception.

Why 432 Hz Carrier?

The carrier tone uses 432 Hz, long associated with natural harmonic tuning, grounded resonance, and heart-centered awareness. It is chosen for its gentle quality and compatibility with meditative states.

How to Use

  1. Lie down comfortably with stereo headphones
  2. Dim lights or darkness — keep the body warm
  3. Remove distractions
  4. Set an intention: "I open myself to insight and inner guidance."
  5. Allow your body to relax deeply — let thoughts pass without engagement
  6. Follow images, feelings, or impressions without forcing

After the Session

  • Remain still for a few minutes
  • Journal what you experienced
  • Drink water and ground yourself gently
  • Avoid immediate stimulation
  • Integration may continue through dreams and subtle insight over the next day

Frequency of Use

Recommended 2–3 times per week, or as needed for inner inquiry. Balance with physical movement and grounding practices. Overuse may cause disorientation or emotional sensitivity — moderation is key.

Avoid if you
  • Are in active psychological crisis
  • Experience dissociation easily
  • Are under the influence of substances
  • Feel unsafe or unstable

Theta is not a place. It is a state of remembering.
This protocol does not give answers.
It creates the space where answers can arise — not from outside, but from within.

Health & Safety

Do not use if you have
  • Epilepsy or a seizure disorder
  • A pacemaker or medical implant
  • Severe mental health conditions without professional guidance
  • A tendency toward dissociation or psychosis
Use with caution if
  • You are pregnant — consult your physician first
  • You are taking psychiatric medications
  • You have a history of trauma
  • You are under 18 — parental guidance recommended

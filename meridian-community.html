Community Standards

This is a space for genuine reflection and respectful exchange. The following are not welcome here and will be removed immediately upon report:

  • Negativity, combative language, or hostility toward others
  • Spam or self-promotion of any kind
  • Off-topic content unrelated to the protocols or inner exploration
  • Misinformation about The Meridian or its protocols
  • Disrespectful treatment of any person or perspective

If you encounter content that violates these standards, use the Report button. It will be hidden immediately and reviewed.

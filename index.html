About The Meridian

A collection of neuro-acoustic binaural audio protocols for meditation, focus, and inner exploration.

Built through long-term personal experimentation with sound, attention, and reflection, The Meridian is a practical system designed to guide attention, deepen meditation, and support personal exploration of consciousness.

  • Neuroscience
  • Meditation
  • Sound design
  • Historical practices
  • Personal inquiry

Rather than offering belief systems or promises, The Meridian offers structured listening experiences that users can explore and evaluate for themselves. It is offered as a tool — not a belief system.
